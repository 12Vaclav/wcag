Adapting Text

AA

Proposed

This is a proposed Success Criterion that has not yet been formally approved by the Working Group. Discussion of the issue is available in Issues 79, 78, and 74 and in Pull request 124. To file comments on this proposal, please raise new issues for each discrete comment in GitHub.

Each of the following text styles of the page can be overridden with no loss of essential content or functionality.

  1. font family by at least one different font family
  2. foreground color by at least one different foreground color
  3. background color by at least one different background color
  4. line spacing (leading) to at least 1.5
  5. letter spacing (tracking) to at least 0.12 em
  6. word spacing to at least 0.16 em

Captions and images of text are exempt.

NOTE: The goal is that pages also work with more fonts, colors, and larger spacing than the minimums cited, but the SC is met if the "at least" value is met.
