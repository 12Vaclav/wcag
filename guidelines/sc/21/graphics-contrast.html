The visual presentation of non-text content has a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 against adjacent color(s) for each of the following: (Level AA)

Controls
Visual information used to indicate state for active user interface components that have been modified by the author.
Graphical objects
Parts of graphics required to understand the content, except when a particular presentation of graphics is essential to the information being conveyed.
