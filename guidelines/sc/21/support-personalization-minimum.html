Support Personalization

A

Proposed

Contextual information is available for essential functionality and content. Essential functionality and content can be programmatically identified.

Exception is available when identifying essential functionality and content, and setting contextual information cannot be achieved through author settable properties or metadata.

note

Additional definitions (if we like it): Essential functionality and content - the mimimum functionality and content that is essential to identify the topic and fulfill the purpose of the content. The purpose, or topic of the content should be identified in the page title (See 2.4.2 and 2.4.6)
